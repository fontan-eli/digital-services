Who We Are

My name is Eileen, and I live right here in San Diego.

My Team and I help people fix their phones, computers, and everyday electronics, but more importantly, We help make things simple and stress free. We know that dealing with technology can sometimes feel overwhelming, and it's not always easy to know who to trust.

That's why We do things differently. Our mission is to bridge the gap between you and technology.We take our time, to explain everything in plain language, and We only recommend what you truly need, nothing more. No pressure, no confusing terms, and no surprises.

I've worked in technology startups, military and enterprise companies for over 25 years, so I've seen just about everything. But what matters most to me is not just fixing the device it's making sure you feel comfortable, respected, and confident in the process.

We are local, We are available, and We stand behind our work. Most of our clients come through word of mouth, and we take that trust very seriously.

If something isn't working the way it should, We're here to help and We'll treat your device as if it were our own.
